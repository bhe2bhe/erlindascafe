OUR STORY

Erlinda's Cafe, initially just a small eatery, was born during the challenging times of the pandemic. The cafe is the brainchild of Bernard and Jeniffer, a dedicated couple with a passion for bringing comfort and joy through food.


Bernard, who previously worked as a store manager at a well-known cafe, brought his extensive experience in the food and beverage industry to their new venture.


Facing the uncertainty of the pandemic, Jennifer saw an opportunity to create something meaningful and lasting. She proposed starting a small restaurant and named it Erlinda's Cafe in honor of her late mother.


Their inaugural menu was a delightful mix of comforting dishes, featuring 'silogs', 'lugaw', and the flavorful chicken biryani.


Bernard and Jennifer's story is a testament to resilience and the power of following one's passion, even in the toughest times.
